About film festival

Rules and regulations

  • Entries for the Filmingo International Short Film Festival are open for everyone. Participants under the legal age must submit a consent letter from their parent or guardian.
  • The Short Film can be based on any subject or concept. We are currently not accepting Animated and/or Documentary Films.
  • The short film can be in any language. However, all films must include sub-titles in English.
  • The total runtime of the Short Film, including opening & closing credits, should strictly be a minimum of 3 minutes and a maximum of 20 minutes.
